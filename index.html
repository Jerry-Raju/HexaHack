Instructions

Participants can take part individually or form a team. The maximum number of members allowed in a team is 6.

A detailed ppt with abstract should be submitted. Click view complete Instructions button to see the format

The team members need not necessarily be from the same institution, cross institution participation is encouraged.

The team formed should have a unique team name that needs to be shared with the organisers on request.

Participants can be undergraduates/postgraduates, preferably pursuing degrees in Computer Related Fields from Kerala.

Click apply with devfolio button in home to register
